Fitness, your way

What is MrFit?

MrFit is an app designed to help you keep track of your workout progress and goals. Build a template of your ideal exercises, keep track of your current and past workouts, meet your goals!

How does it work?

Check out our demo, made by the developers themselves! It's as simple as hand-crafting your desired workout and hitting start. First pick from any number of exercises, or make one of your own for you and other users of MrFit to enjoy. Then customize them to fit your workout needs. When you're done, just hit start to begin your workout! We've even included a handy checklist to keep track of your progress

Helping you reach your goals

MrFit is an app that wants to be there to help you! Rather than bog you down with achievements and goals you don't care about, we let you define your own. Let us keep track of the things that are important to you, and when you've accomplished your goal we'll be there to celebrate with you!

Fitness on your time

You and your schedule are unique, plan your workouts at time that work best for you. Choose the month, day, and year as well as the specific time you want to be reminded. Use our built-in calendar to keep track of which workout you are doing for the day.

Really liked that workout? Do it again!

In this screen, the user is able to schedule a workout reminder. They can choose the month, day, and year as well as the specific time they want to be reminded. It is a great feature that helps users remember which workout they are doing for the day.

In this screen, the user is able to start their workout for the specific exercises they want to do. Once they press the Start Workout button, a timer starts. After they finish and check off their workouts, they click the Finish Workout button where they are told the time they finished their workout.

This screen is the user's workout history. Here, they are able to track all of their past workouts. They can view the specific exercise, the date, and the time. The user can also delete their past workouts if they wanted to.

MrFit is an app designed to help you keep track of your workout progress and goals. Build a template of your ideal exercises, keep track of your current and past workouts, meet your goals!

Your Goals

MrFit is an app that wants to be there to help you! Rather than bog you down with achievements and goals you don't care about, we let you define your own. Let us keep track of the things that are important to you, and when you've accomplished your goal we'll be there to celebrate with you!
